I'm a Marie Curie Fellow at the GRavitation AstroParticle Physics Amsterdam (GRAPPA) Center at the University of Amsterdam. My research focuses on revealing the particle nature of dark matter using observational probes such as gamma rays and other high-energy particles.  I'm an Affiliated Scientist in the Fermi Gamma-ray Space Telescope collaboration, and a member of the Cherenkov Telescope Array (CTA) Consortium.

You can find me on twitter @jmgaskins.
